About Me

Hey, I'm Michał—when I'm not building beautiful and functional web apps, I'm probably lost in the world of imagination.

In my spare time, I design epic adventures for my Dungeons & Dragon campaign with friends. Between sessions, you'll find me oil painting dark fantasy landscapes or finishing them off with a haunting guitar riff or a moody piano tune.

I'm also working on an indie video game called Reviclades— a creative, elemental-fueled world built with a bunch of friends who love storytelling and strategy.

For business and client work, I co-run a boutique software house called Nosiadek Productions where we turn ideas into sleek digital products. Check us out!
